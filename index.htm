Engagement

23 June 2018

Love is a book in which ‘engagement’ is the romantic chapter that gives you a glimpse of the beautiful journey that lies ahead.

We realised on our engagement day that everything in our life comes down to just three things- You, our love and Us. And this is a realisation that we’ll carry over to our married life too. In fact, it is the feeling that would lead us to live harmonious life even with all the ups and downs.

marriage

The fact that you want to spend your entire life with someone and be a part of their everyday life means that you are ready to share everything, compromise somewhere, and agree to trust your partner.
